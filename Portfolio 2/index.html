Easton Heroux

Broadcast & Digital Journalism Student | Syracuse University

Welcome to my portfolio. I'm Easton Heroux, a sophomore at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University, studying Broadcast and Digital Journalism with a minor in Sport Management. I'm passionate about storytelling, sports media, and bringing compelling stories to life through video, audio, and digital content.

"If everything was easy in life what would be the point." - My Mom

Beyond the classroom, I serve as a Manager for the Syracuse University Men's Soccer Team, where I support day-to-day operations and gain firsthand experience in collegiate athletics. I'm also an active member of WAER, Syracuse's award-winning radio station, where I develop my on-air presence and sharpen my skills in sports broadcasting and journalism.

Whether it's behind a microphone at WAER, on the sidelines with the Orange soccer program, or working on my next story, I'm always looking for ways to grow as a journalist and storyteller. Feel free to explore my work and get in touch — I'd love to connect.
