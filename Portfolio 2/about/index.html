About Me

Broadcast Journalism Major at Syracuse University with a Minor in Sport Management

My name is Easton Heroux, and I'm a sophomore at Syracuse University studying Broadcast Journalism with a minor in Sport Management. I grew up in Middletown, Rhode Island, where my love for sports really took shape. That passion is ultimately what led me to pursue a career in the sports industry. I chose Syracuse for its renowned journalism program and its rich athletic tradition, and so far it's been a great fit. I've been able to build some real experience through internships and extracurricular activities, and I'm looking forward to continuing to grow and develop as I work toward my goals in the industry.

Outside of the classroom, I keep pretty busy. I serve as a manager for the men's soccer team, broadcast for Le Moyne Athletics, and work the talk desk at WAER. I'm also the president of the club pickleball team, which has been a fun way to stay connected to the sports world outside of my more professional roles. Through all of these experiences, I've been able to build some real hands-on experience, and I'm looking forward to continuing to grow as I work toward my goals in the industry.
